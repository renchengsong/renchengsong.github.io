BSIPL-motion dataset

Biomedical Signal and Image Processing Laboratory Motion Scenario Dataset

Download

Download the request from , print it, sign it and send it to rcsong@hfui.edu.cn. Once received, we will share you a download link. The size of the database is approx. 272 GB. It is distributed in a single file compressed with a 7zip program.

Description

Dataset acquisition setup

Subject: 33 subjects (26 male, 7 female) with age range 21 to 25 years
Camera: RGB Logitech C920 camera - 30fps, 640x480 pixels stored in an uncompressed YUV planar pixel format(approx. 1GB per viedo)
Reference signal: Contec CMS50E pulse oximeter - 60hz PPG signal
Lighting setup: 1) natural light coming from a nearby window. 2) two 155W LED with a color temperature of 3200K
Activities: 

  • Speaking : The participants were seated in front of a camera. The cards are situated in front of the subjects, specifically at the central, left-front, and right-front positions. A section of prompt words, scrambled in order, was written on each card. The tasks for the participants were to identify the correct sequence of the text and read it aloud. This scenario involved both head movements and facial expression changes.
  • Anterior-posterior Movement : Participants were instructed to perform forward and backward movements, changing the distance between their faces and the camera. They were instructed to move a minimum of 20 centimeters, with a speed of no less than 45 movements per minute.
  • Horizontal Movement Scenario: Participants were instructed to perform horizontal body movements, with a minimum speed of no less than 45 times per minute.
  • Random Rotation : Participants were instructed to relax and perform random head rotations.

Manual

AVI/
||---XX/
||-------YY/
||------------ZZ/
||---------------video.avi
||---------------wave.csv

where XX is the subject ID, YY is the light ID, ,ZZ is the activity ID and file is one of {"video.avi", "wave.csv"}. XX = {00,01,...,16}.

YY - lighting setup
0 - nature light
1 - LED lighting

ZZ - ativity
0 - Speaking
1 - Anterior-posterior Movement
2 - Horizontal Movement
3 - Random Rotation

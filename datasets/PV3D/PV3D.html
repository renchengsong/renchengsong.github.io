PV3D Dataset

Physiological-assisted Visual Deception Detection Dataset

Description

We propose a multi-modal video deception detection dataset, named PV3D, through designing a simulated theft experiment scenario. The dataset includes video, audio, heart rate, respiration, blood volume pulse (BVP) signal, and blood oxygen saturation. A total of 101 students participated in the experiment, among which 48 subjects lied, and 53 subjects told the truth. Specifically, video data were captured using Logitech C920 HD Pro cameras at a resolution of 640*480, with a frame rate of 30 frames per second (FPS). BVP signals, heart rate, and respiration signals were recorded synchronously using Contec CMS50E pulse oximeters and HKH-11C respiratory sensors. To our knowledge, this is the first dataset that can be simultaneously used for video deception detection, remote heart rate monitoring, and remote respiration monitoring. Notice: Only data from 96 subjects were used in our study. S97、S98、S99、S100、S101 are supplementary data.
